Welcome to Global Gors Association (GGA) GLOBAL GORS ASSOCIATION(GGA) is to provide a platform for all the like-minded, passionate and committed people who understand and share the culture, heritage and values of Gor community, to participate in its socio-economic development. Preserve, maintain, and perpetuate the cultural heritage of Gor community in US and abroad. Collaborate with other organizations across the globe to promote the cultural, literacy, educational and charitable interests of Gor community. Foster friendship and understanding among Gor community. Raise, solicit, collect, and disburse funds, charities, and donations for cultural, educational, and charitable purposes either directly or in cooperation with other not-for-profit organizations in the US and abroad.
