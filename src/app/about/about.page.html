About Us
Group of like-minded individuals have come together to establish this charitable trust with a broad focus mainly on Education, Culture and Livelihood. At “GLOBAL GORS ASSOCIATION”, our goal is to create a social network for all members to come together as One Big Team to celebrate our unique culture, whether it’s a happy hour, fitness activity or an opportunity for professional or personal development. When you join the GGA family, you are guided by a set of core values that have become the foundation of our character, success and global reputation.
Our Mission
To create a platform for individuals and families to collaborate on improving the lives of marginalized and holistic development of Gor communities in India with basic education, digital literacy, Skill development, health awareness, learning modern and rural entrepreneurship.
Our Vision
Get all Global Gors (irrespective of their Religion / State / Nation affiliations) to form a coherent community in US and abroad and to give back to the community back in Sub-Continent.
Our Foundation
Gors aims to nurture and support this community, recognizing that any Gor can be part of it, whether their engagement with culture is through festivals, cultural activities or family gatherings to retain unique identify of our culture for future generations. People working in GGA team are Engineers, Doctors, Entrepreneurs, Teachers and etc., but when they all came together to work on a GGA platform they all works as a team to achieve a common goal of our cultural heritage keeps our memories alive of how beliefs and cultural expressions have contributed to the development of family and society.
